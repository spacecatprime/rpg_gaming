In her heart, she is a crusader who seeks justice for those she has determined betrayed. She will not standby if her family or friends are threatened. She will seek vengeance if she has been betrayed.

She fears pitch black darkness even though she can see in darkness. Indeed, her rod cells are more sensitive giving her natural lowlight vision, but even elves cannot see in pure darkness! This fear combines with the dread of being alone; she is a creature that needs companionship.

She loose and easy-going with companions that have earned her trust. She is a bit awkward with new people and large crowds, unless she is talking about her inventions or business. It turns out she is quite a skilled businessperson with a talent to market her arms business and clever gadgets.

She is most comfortable when she is in her workshop problem solving, testing her newest gadgets, or blowing up something with her awesome unique arsenal.
DESCRIPTION Show: YESPage Break: NO
She started her gadget invention business in her parent’s garage. She was fascinated by all the tinkering in robotics, tech-gadgets, and weapons. Her clever weapon designed got her noticed when she won a science fair by inventing a working prototype of a plasma launcher in the form of a pistol; she was only 18 years old.
Anton Wagner, a fledging inventor businessman, convinced Alison to come work in his workshop to continue her work; he claimed she would be a full partner. Being young and excited about inventing for a living, she took the job where she worked for a few years until she discovered he was stealing her ideas instead of giving her credit. After discovering his betrayal, she left his company to form her own. In fact, she continues to sue him for breach of contract and industrial espionage to get her designs back.

Her family life is much more stable and happier. She created her own workshop in the basement of a suburban house outside of Seattle to run her legal arms dealing business. She lives with her younger sister (Lia) and cats. Her parents still live in Vancouver, Canada but the family is still close and supportive.
She has found a talent for running in the shadows as well. Recently she has been going on a few secret missions in corporate espionage to help those small businesses that she feels are being unfairly squeezed by the governments and mega-corporations.
