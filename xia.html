The woman with many faces, or that is what the rumors say about the enigmatic occult investigator solving hard to crack mysteries. Xia does not usually use her true identity to run in the shadows, instead she uses a variety of personalities that she has been cultivating for years. She is a talented sleuth using her magic skills (spirits to track) and perception abilities to sniff out those hard to see details.

She loves going on missions, charming targets to find obscure, hard to find targets. She finds honest pleasure in a job well done. She sincerely cares about each of her personas and wants them all to succeed, but her favorite persona is Cinder. She is very calm and easily switches personas to align with the situation.

She is known a “X” by her close friends. Her shaman mentor is the closet person that knows most about her, but she has trust issues with almost all other people.
In her free time she like to likes to hang out in parks near nature and a nice stroll through an art house of festival. But when she is an urban environment she become fidgety when she has nothing to do; drives her to get the next mission.

She has ultra-strong ideas about death and killing. Her shamanic training stressed honoring life above most else, she held this belief so strong that a Bear spirit mentor attached itself to her. She hates seeing death and suffering; she will not kill a living creature unless she is forced to defend herself.

Her father died of an illness while she was early elementary school. Xia was orphaned at the age of ten. This basically shaped her entire life since she witnessed the death of her own mother; to make things worse her mother was a famous person so she was constantly reminded of this tragic event until she started coming up with her alterative identities so that she would not have to be reminded all the time.

The system split up Payton (her sister) and her to foster families who meant well but Xia never truly caught on with the family. Instead she would stay away from the home in the darkness of San Francisco’s nights learning the streets, hanging out with gangs, and learn how to survive on the street. At the age of 16 she just stopped coming home and hung out at wherever she found kindness and kinship, a shaman’s lair. There she felt out true kinship with the mystical arts and spry spirits whimsically flying around.

At the age of 17 she started using her sleuthing skills and magic to start hunting down her little sister. As it turns out she found her in the San Francisco city, but on the nice side of town near the Silicon Valley. Payton was very happy with the adopted family that she was assigned so she no reason to leave, but they promised to keep in touch.
Xia became a sought out “detective in the shadows” for certain punk rocker type of shadow runners. She became romantically involved with Darius, a member of one of the groups. She had been very upfront with the group about her shamanic belief system, no killing.

They mostly did search-and-retrieve missions for the most part, but one mission went sideways, and Xia was asked to kill a guard to protect the team instead of aborting the mission. She refused the order which caused the group to fail the mission.
Unfortunately for her, this mission was critical to their secret employer who happened by the powerful mega-corporation Aztechnology. In order to get back in good graces, the leader of the group pegged Xia as the reason the mission failed then setup Xia for an take down with the police. The leader was Darius.
Xia barely escaped using her magic, many faces, and wits. Presently she is moving to a new city to city to avoid the Aztechnology corp, which lead her to Seattle.
